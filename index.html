Game Rules

  • Choose your card and click on an attribute to compete.
  • If your value is higher, you win the round and take the opponent's card.
  • If you lose, the computer wins the round.
  • If it's a tie, the same player plays again.
  • The winner is the one who collects all the cards first.
  • In the "Rank in UK" attribute, the lower the number, the better the result.
